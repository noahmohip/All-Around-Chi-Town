Frequently Asked

The booth platform is approximately 6ft in diameter and the arm extends about 6ft horizontally. We recommend a minimum 10x10ft open area for the best experience and to give guests room to move around.

We recommend booking at least 2–3 weeks in advance, especially for weekends and holiday events. Popular dates fill up fast — use the contact form below to check availability for your date.

Videos are sent instantly via text or QR code the moment they're recorded. Guests can download and share directly to TikTok, Instagram, or anywhere else right from the platform. Every video is also backed up digitally so nothing is ever lost.

Yes, always. Every booking includes a dedicated on-site attendant who handles setup, runs the booth, helps guests, and ensures everything runs smoothly from start to finish. You don't have to worry about a thing.

We serve Chicago and all surrounding suburbs including Oak Park, Evanston, Naperville, Schaumburg, Joliet, Aurora, and more — all with free delivery. Not sure if we cover your area? Reach out and we'll let you know.

We require a deposit to hold your date. Cancellations made more than 7 days before the event receive a full refund of the deposit. Cancellations within 7 days are non-refundable but can be rescheduled within 60 days at no extra charge.

The platform comfortably fits 1–4 people per session. Groups work great — the more people, the more energy and the better the video. There is no limit on the number of sessions, so everyone at your event gets a turn.

Absolutely. Custom overlays are one of our most popular add-ons. We'll brand every video with your name, event date, logo, or any design you like. Just mention it when you fill out the booking form and we'll take care of the rest.
