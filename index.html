How It Works

Three simple steps to the most unforgettable video of your life.

01
Step On The Platform

Guests step onto our elevated platform, strike a pose, and let our on-site attendant guide the whole experience.

02
The Arm Spins Around You

Our HD camera arm rotates a full 360° — choose normal speed for a clean look or slow-motion for a cinematic, jaw-dropping effect.

Your video is processed instantly and sent straight to your phone. Every clip is digitally backed up — no memory is ever lost.
